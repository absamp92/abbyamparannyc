About

Hi, I'm Abby.

I'm a hairstylist, colorist, educator, and brand ambassador with over 15 years of experience helping people feel confident through beautiful, customized hair.

My career has taken me from salon floors to editorial projects, advanced education programs, and working alongside some of the most respected brands in the beauty industry. Today, I proudly serve clients in both New York City and San Antonio while representing Maria Nila as a member of their Pro Team.

I specialize in lived-in blondes, balayage, color corrections, extensions, and precision haircuts designed to complement each client's lifestyle and individuality.

When I'm not behind the chair, you'll usually find me running scenic trails, playing tennis, traveling, exploring new cities, spending time with family and friends, or enjoying a great conversation over coffee. I believe beauty, confidence, wellness, and personal growth all go hand in hand.

Whether you're looking for a complete transformation, subtle enhancement, or simply a stylist who truly listens, I'm honored you're here and I look forward to meeting you.
